# Interviewing at a startup

**Author:** [[Paige Omura]]  
**Source:** [YC Library](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/F2-interviewing-at-a-startup)

## Summary

At YC, we tell our founders to share details about their interview process with candidates. This brings transparency and
a better job hunting experience for job seekers. It also helps candidates do better on the interview, which benefits
everyone.
