## Summary

The Social Radars Podcast: The Social Radars (Jessica Livingston and Carolynn Levy) talk with Parker Conrad, who founded two startups that went on to become unicorns: Zenefits in 2013, and Rippling in 2016. Parker’s story is one the more dramatic ones you’ll hear on this podcast.
