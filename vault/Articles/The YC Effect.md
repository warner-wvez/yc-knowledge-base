# The YC Effect

**Author:** [[Oleg Rogynskyy]]  
**Source:** [YC Library](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/4i-the-yc-effect)

## Summary

As the founder of a startup that graduated from Y Combinator I'm often asked about my experience - particularly by other founders. Was it valuable? What did we get out of it? What did we learn? Y Combinator is about understanding the metrics you need to hit to grow and thrive, how to be laser-focused on your company's goals and lots and lots of hard work.
