## Summary

Design Review: YC’s Aaron Epstein sat down with Framer co-founder and CEO Jorn van Dijk to review user-submitted websites built using Framer. They share practical advice on how to clearly communicate what your product does, when animation helps (and when it hurts), and how to use Framer’s powerful tools without ending up with a site that feels generic or overly templatized.

## YouTube Description

Jorn van Dijk is the CEO and co-founder of Framer, one of the most influential website design platforms in the world. As a designer-turned-founder, Jorn has helped shape how modern startups think about the power of design when telling their stories. In this episode of Design Review, YC’s Aaron Epstein sat down with Jorn to review user-submitted websites built using Framer. They'll share practical advice on how to clearly communicate what your product does, when animation helps (and when it hurts), and how to use Framer’s powerful tools without ending up with a site that feels generic or overly templatized.
