## Summary

Embark Trucks (W16), self-driving semi trucks, went public.

## YouTube Description

In this episode, the founders of Embark Trucks (YC W16), Alex Rodrigues and Brandon Moak, catch up with YC President Geoff Ralston. Alex and Brandon share their evolution from Varden Labs, building self-driving shuttles for use on campuses, to building self-driving semi trucks with Embark Trucks. They also reflect on their time at YC, their learnings building a publicly-traded, multi-billion dollar, hard-tech company, and share their advice for new founders.
