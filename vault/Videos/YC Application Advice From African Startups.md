## Summary

YC founders from African startups share advice for founders applying to YC, including overcoming fear of applying, what YC looks for when selecting companies, what to do if you applied before, and more.

## YouTube Description

Dimeji Sofowora, co-founder of Nigerian Y Combinator startup Helium Health, provides some helpful advice for African founders who're considering applying to YC. At the Y Combinator Lagos Meetup, 9 Nigerian and Ghanaian YC alum companies shared stories from their YC experience, as well as advice about putting together a strong YC application.
