Founder Stories: Ooshma Garg, the founder and CEO of dinner kit delivery service Gobble (YC W14), details her company's complicated years-long path to what might look from the outside like simple success, and described how building a startup is like a daily game of Snakes and Ladders that can take you two steps forward, and then two steps back.
