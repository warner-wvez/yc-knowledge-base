## Summary

Pivoting: David Lieb, founder of Bump (YC S09) and former head of Google Photos, talks about his journey as a founder and how Bump became one of the biggest apps on the App Store. He discusses how Bump pivoted from contact sharing to photo app, and his transition to scaling today's biggest photo app, Google Photos.
