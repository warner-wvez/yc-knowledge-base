# Notice and attribution

## Source material

All source material indexed in this repository belongs to **Y Combinator** and the individual authors and speakers. It originates from the [Y Combinator Startup Library](https://www.ycombinator.com/library).

This repository is a personal, non-commercial study index. It contains:

- Note titles
- Structured metadata (author, series, dates, links, tags)
- Short summaries and original descriptions
- The link graph that connects authors, series, and entries
- Generated index maps

It does **not** contain the full video transcripts or the full text of the articles. Every note links back to its original source on ycombinator.com so the real material is always one click away.
